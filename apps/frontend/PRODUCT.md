# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Power users tracking their own finances locally on desktop. Opens Zai to see net worth, cash flow, and spending without cloud sync or bank-login friction. Context: focused session at a desk, often alongside spreadsheets or statements — wants clarity fast, not onboarding theater.

## Product Purpose

Zai is a personal finance desktop app (Tauri + local SQLite) for self-directed money tracking: net worth, transactions, categories, and settings. Success means the user trusts the numbers, finds the primary task on any screen in seconds, and never fights the UI to enter or review data.

## Brand Personality

Confident minimal — expert tool energy, dense when it earns it, always legible. Voice is direct and calm; UI stays out of the way of the data. Green accent signals growth and clarity without fintech hype.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards: identical card grids, gradient heroes, eyebrow kickers, hero-metric templates
- Decorative motion and glass chrome that slow comprehension
- Marketing-page patterns inside the app shell (scroll storytelling, oversized display type on util screens)

## Design Principles

1. **Numbers first** — hierarchy serves the figure or trend the user came to see; decoration never competes with data.
2. **Confident restraint** — Copilot Money / Linear density and calm; one accent, tight spacing rhythm, no visual noise.
3. **Local trust** — surfaces feel precise and durable; errors and empty states are explicit, never hand-wavy.
4. **Simple flows** — prefer obvious paths over clever UX; fewer steps beats impressive interactions.
5. **Desktop-native** — keyboard, sidebar, and table patterns that respect a mouse-first, large-screen context.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA baseline: contrast on body and chart labels, visible focus, semantic structure. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for any non-essential animation.
